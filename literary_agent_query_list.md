## Query Strategy Notes

**Batch 1 (send first):** Agents 1-5 (Tier 1) — strongest alignment with the specific intersection of South Asian diaspora + literary fiction

**Batch 2 (send 2-3 weeks later):** Agents 6-10 (Tier 2) — proven track record with debut literary fiction exploring identity in unconventional forms

**Batch 3 (send after refining based on Batch 1 feedback):** Agents 11-16 (Tier 3) — strong literary fiction agents who are building or diversifying their lists

**Monitor:** Agents 17-20 — check quarterly for reopened submissions; pursue referral paths through writing community

**Key selling points to emphasize across all queries:**
- Mixed-media format (epistolary/found document tradition reinvented for social media age)
- Male South Asian protagonist (underrepresented perspective in diaspora fiction)
- Tech industry insider writing literary fiction (authentic platform knowledge)
- Comp: "Normal People for the age of infinite scroll"
- Themes of digital intimacy and control resonate with current cultural moment
