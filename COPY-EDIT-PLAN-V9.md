## Pass 3: Grammar & Sentence-Level

Focus areas based on the manuscript's style:

1. **Comma splices** — The manuscript uses intentional comma splices as a voice choice (run-on confessional rhythm). Flag only the ones that read as errors vs. those that read as rhythm.
2. **Fragment consistency** — Fragments are the dominant style. Don't "fix" them. Only flag fragments that feel unintentional.
3. **Tense consistency** — Ari narrates in present tense ("I sit," "she says") with past tense for backstory. Verify no slippage in transitions.
4. **Pronoun clarity** — In sections with two "she" characters (Meera + Priya, or Meera + daughter), verify the referent is always clear.
5. **Repetition audit** — Flag words used 3+ times in the same paragraph (intentional repetition is a device in this manuscript, so judgment required).
