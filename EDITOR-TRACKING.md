## Decision Criteria

Before hiring, confirm:
- [ ] They've edited at least 2–3 full-length literary fiction manuscripts
- [ ] They can articulate what a developmental edit includes (not line editing)
- [ ] Sample edit of first 3 chapters feels insightful (not just surface praise)
- [ ] Turnaround time works (4–6 weeks)
- [ ] Rate is within $800–$1,500
- [ ] They understand mixed-media/unconventional narrative formats
